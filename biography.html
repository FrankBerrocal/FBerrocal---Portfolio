Biography

"Inspire others..."

I am a human being in constant change, in permanent evolution. This quality has taken me through unexpected unknown roads. It has led me to meet extraordinary people everywhere around the world.

In retrospect, music has always been constant. I started learning how to play the flute at a young age, trying to decode 36 keys years later; adding more scales during my adolescent years. After that, strings from the bass and the guitar. Always struggling to find the best instrument, and even so, vocal cords were never part of my repertoire.

It was then, shortly after my forties, that I discovered my voice. I realized something: my deep voice has a purpose. And even more important than that: I liked my voice. I listened to others with similar voices also, singing notes that are centuries old. I fell in love with Opera because my voice fits, and I can be whoever I want: in Opera, my voice is strong, has a message, coherence and destiny. My muse has a thousand faces but only one soul.

I have had roles as a computer engineer, marketeer, project manager; but I don’t doubt it: my life’s third act will develop in the stage and everything related to it. My biggest desire is being able to inspire people with my voice.

Let’s sing.

Artistic Resume

Started formal technical studies in lyric singing in January 2018, accompanied by Maestro Yeancarlo Zamora, and after August 2018 with Maestro Danilo Chavez1 Participated in acting workshops directed by late actress Sara Astica, as part of the cultural extension programs of Universidad Latinoamericana de Ciencia y Tecnología.

His repertoire includes lieder, Italian song, Russian song, and opera. " «La Cambiale di Matrimonio» (G. Rossini)2,3 was his overall debut as a lyric singer in Costa Rica for the Rosso Artístico company (July 2018, March 2019) in the role of Norton.

Frank presented his first solo concert in Heredia and Santa Ana (Escuela Municipal de Artes Integradas4,5) in September 2019 (with material from Rossini, Mozart, Wagner, Tchaikovsky, Gounod, Bellini, among others). In February 2020, attended an interpretation workshop, facilitated by Silvia Baltodano (La Colmena Community Art6).

During the isolation period 2020-late 2021, he was working on vocal score transcriptions, Russian and German language, and preparing new repertoire with greater emphasis in Russian music (after cancellations of a sacred music concert in April and a third solo concert in May).

Currently he is singing with the Calgary Men´s Choir, practicing his technique at Hillhurst United Church, and in the active search for a classic vocal coach in Calgary.
